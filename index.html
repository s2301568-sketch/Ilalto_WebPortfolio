about me

I'm a creative who enjoys blending design, storytelling, and emotion into visual work. Most of my projects are inspired by modern internet culture, indie aesthetics, music, and everyday moments that usually go unnoticed.

I like making designs that feel expressive instead of overly perfect—something memorable, human, and honest.

Currently exploring graphic design, branding, photography, and creative direction. I'm also exploring other forms of art, mainly theater, which continues to shape the way I create and tell stories.

I occasionally draw too—small sketches and ideas that quietly fuel my creative drive.
